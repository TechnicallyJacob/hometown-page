Visit Cookeville

Enjoy history and nature in this cozy city in Middle Tennessee.

Top three activities to do in Cookeville

Burgess Falls State Park is a natural area southwest of Cookeville featuring a series of waterfalls, including a dramatic 136-foot drop along the Falling Water River and scenic trails through rugged gorge terrain.

The historic Cookeville Railroad Depot, built in 1909 and listed on the National Register of Historic Places, now houses the Cookeville Depot Museum showcasing Tennessee Central Railroad artifacts and exhibits.

Tennessee Technological University is a public research university in Cookeville known for its engineering, technology, and STEM programs, with a campus community of over 10,000 students.
